Message from the Secretary-General

Esteemed Delegates,

It is my distinct honour to welcome you to the inaugural GSV Model United Nations 2026. In an era defined by rapid technological advancement and complex geopolitical tensions, the need for thoughtful diplomatic dialogue has never been greater.

Youth participation remains the cornerstone of meaningful and sustainable global policymaking. At GSV MUN, we strive to create a platform where rigorous debate meets constructive resolution, and where diverse perspectives contribute to deeper understanding.

Whether you are a seasoned delegate or stepping into a committee room for the first time, your voice and ideas matter. I look forward to witnessing the diplomacy, negotiations, and innovative solutions you bring to the floor.

May this conference inspire dialogue, diplomacy, and the pursuit of solutions that transcend borders.

Parth Sidhu

Secretary-General, GSV MUN 2026
