Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Model United Nations?

It is an educational simulation where students learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations by acting as delegates for different countries.

Who can participate in GSV MUN?

GSV MUN is open to students participating in the EPITOME 2026 fest.

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all! This event is highly beginner-friendly, and the Executive Board will guide new delegates through the rules of procedure.

What committee will be simulated?

We will be simulating the United Nations General Assembly – Disarmament and International Security Committee (UNGA DISEC).

What is the agenda?

The agenda is: Addressing the Rise of Hybrid Warfare and Escalating Regional Conflicts (Proxy Warfare, Cross-Border Terrorism, Cyber Operations, and Disinformation Campaigns).

How will country portfolios be assigned?

Portfolios will be assigned based on your preferences in the registration form and availability. Registering early gives you a better chance of getting your preferred country.

What is the participation fee?

The delegate participation fee is ₹49.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes, all delegates will receive a certificate of participation.

Are there prizes?

Details regarding prizes for outstanding delegates will be informed later subject to approval by the college administration.

What should I wear?

Western Formals or Indian Formals are expected. Dressing professionally enhances the diplomatic atmosphere.

What happens during committee sessions?

Delegates will give speeches outlining their country's stance, debate specific topics, form alliances, and collaboratively draft working papers to solve the issues on the agenda.

Is there a limit on participants?

Yes, registrations close on 8 March, 11:59 p.m., and are strictly subject to slot availability. Early registration is recommended.
